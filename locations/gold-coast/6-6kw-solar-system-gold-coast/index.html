Government Solar Incentives for a 6.6kW Solar System Gold Coast

Australia offers solar incentives to encourage more homeowners to switch to renewable energy. These incentives significantly reduce the upfront cost of solar installation services.

The main solar incentive available is the Small-Scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES), which provides Small-Scale Technology Certificates (STCs) based on the expected energy production of your system. These STCs can be traded for a discount, lowering the cost of your 6.6kW Solar System Gold Coast by thousands of dollars.
