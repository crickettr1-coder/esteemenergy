What Are Solar Installation Rebates?

Solar installation rebates are government programs that reduce the cost of installing solar energy systems for Australian homes and businesses. These rebates, such as Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs), allow you to claim financial credits for the clean energy your system will produce.
