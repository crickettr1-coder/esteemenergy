How a 5kW Solar System Works

A 5kW solar system installation generates power through the following process:

  • Solar Panels Capture Sunlight – Photovoltaic (PV) cells in the panels absorb sunlight and convert it into DC electricity.
  • Solar Inverter Converts DC to AC – The 5kW inverter transforms DC into AC power, which can be used by home appliances.
  • Electricity Powers the Home – The converted electricity runs your lights, appliances, and electronic devices.
  • Excess Energy is Fed into the Grid – Surplus electricity can be exported to the grid, earning credits through feed-in tariffs.
