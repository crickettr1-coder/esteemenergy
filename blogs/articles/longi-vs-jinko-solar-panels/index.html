Technology Comparison

PERC vs Mono: What’s the Difference?

Understanding PERC vs Mono technology is key in the longi vs jinko solar panels decision.

  • Monocrystalline (LONGi):

    • Made from a single silicon crystal

    • Typically offers higher efficiency

    • Performs well in low-light conditions

    • Known for longevity and premium aesthetics

  • PERC (Jinko):

    • Passivated Emitter Rear Cell technology

    • Adds a reflective layer to improve sunlight capture

    • Increases output during early morning and late afternoon

    • Affordable and widely used in residential systems

While both technologies are effective, monocrystalline panels from LONGi generally offer slightly better efficiency in Australia's climate. However, PERC panels from Jinko provide exceptional value for the price.
