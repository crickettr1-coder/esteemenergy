Strategies for Maximizing Solar Panel Efficiency and Performance

1. Install in Unshaded Areas:

   - One can position solar panels in an unshaded area free from shading to maximize sunlight exposure throughout the day. Shade from trees, buildings, or other obstructions can significantly reduce panel efficiency.

2. Proper & Regular Cleaning:

   -It is advised to clean solar panels occasionally to prevent dust, dirt, and debris from accumulating and obstructing sunlight. It helps maintain peak performance and ensures maximum energy production.

3. Monitor System Output:

   - Keep track of the daily electricity output of your solar panel system using monitoring apps available on many inverters. If you notice any dips in performance, contact your solar installer promptly to diagnose and address any issues that may arise.

4. Professional Installation:

   - Choosing a professional solar company for better solar installation is mandatory to ensure proper wiring and setup. An improper installation can reduce productivity or damage your solar power system. Professional installers can also provide ongoing support and troubleshooting assistance as needed.

Types of Solar Panels:

There are 3 types of panels:

Monocrystalline Panels:

They are expensive compared to others and also highly efficient, made of silicon and have extended durability so they are in high demand. These panels can cut the cost of the system in half and make sure that you are getting solar panel power consumption at maximum possibility.

Polycrystalline Panels:

These Esteem Energy panels are made of melted silicon crystals. They are moderately efficient and priced, but their lifetime is short compared to monocrystalline.
