Government Rebates and Incentives in NSW

Thanks to the Australian Government’s Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES), eligible homeowners can receive upfront discounts that significantly lower the 6.6kW solar system price NSW. These incentives come in the form of Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs) and can reduce your installation cost by up to $3,000, depending on your zone and system size. Here’s what you need to qualify:
  • The system must be installed by a CEC-accredited installer 
  • All equipment must be approved by the Clean Energy Council
  • The property must be eligible under current scheme conditions
These rebates make a substantial difference in the New South Wales solar price, helping more homeowners switch to solar without breaking their budget.
