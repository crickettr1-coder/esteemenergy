What Is a 13.2kW Solar System?

A 13.2kW solar system is considered a large residential solar solution, capable of generating enough electricity to support high daily usage in homes or businesses. This system includes:

  • Around 30 to 36 solar panels, typically rated between 370W and 440W

  • A 10kW or 12kW inverter, depending on the setup

  • Optional battery storage for nighttime usage and backup power

  • A monitoring system to track performance

The 13.2kW system cost is higher than standard systems, but it delivers far more value by generating an estimated 50 to 55 kWh per day, depending on location and weather. That’s roughly 18,000 to 20,000 kWh per year, which is well above average Australian household consumption. It’s also worth considering systems like the 5 kW solar system if your household needs less energy.

This makes it ideal for homes with electric vehicles, ducted air conditioning, home offices, or multiple family members. It’s also suitable for small commercial properties needing a dependable, sustainable energy source.

13.2kW Solar System Price in Australia (2024–2025)

The 13.2kW solar system price in Australia typically ranges between $10,000 and $18,000, depending on several factors. Understanding this range can help you budget realistically for your solar investment.

Average Price Range:

  • Standard quality system without battery: $10,000 – $13,000

  • Premium system with battery storage (10–13kWh): $15,000 – $18,000

  • Premium panel brands (like Trina, Jinko, Risen): Add $1,500 – $2,500 to base cost

The total 13.2kW system cost depends on:

  • Solar panel brand and efficiency

  • Inverter type and warranty

  • Roof size, material, and pitch

  • Location of your property (metro vs. regional)

  • STCs (Small-scale Technology Certificates) and solar rebates

  • Labour and installation complexity

When comparing quotes, always check if the system includes professional installation, grid connection, CEC-accredited components, and monitoring.

Rooftop System Pricing: What Can Impact Your Quote?

Rooftop system pricing varies because every home is different. Several on-site factors will influence the final quote of your 13.2kW solar system price:

  • Roof type and condition – Tiled and steep roofs may cost more to install on than metal or flat roofs.

  • Panel positioning – The amount of direct sunlight your roof gets will impact the system's performance and layout.

  • Shading and roof orientation – Trees, chimneys, and other structures can reduce efficiency if not accounted for.

  • Access and install difficulty – If your roof is hard to access or the system layout is complex, labour costs rise.

  • Inverter location – Longer cable runs between panels and the inverter may also affect pricing.

Energy Output and Performance of a 13.2kW Solar System

One of the biggest advantages of investing in a high-output solar system is its ability to deliver large-scale power generation even in varying weather conditions.

A 13.2kW solar system can produce:

This level of output allows you to:

  • Power high-consumption appliances throughout the day

Homes consuming 25kWh+ daily will benefit the most from this kind of system.

Frequently Asked Questions​

Q1. How much does a 13.2kW solar system cost in Australia?

 The 13.2kW solar system price typically ranges from $10,000 to $18,000, depending on the panel brand, inverter type, roof design, and whether you include battery storage.

Q2. Is a 13.2kW system good for a large home?

Yes, it’s ideal for large homes with high power consumption, electric vehicles, or additional appliances like pool filters and ducted air conditioning.

Q3. What rebates apply for 13.2kW systems?

Eligible households can benefit from STCs (Small-scale Technology Certificates) and state-based rebates, which can reduce the 13.2kW system cost by thousands of dollars.

Q4. Can I install a 13.2kW solar system without a battery?

Absolutely. The system works great without a battery and can export excess energy to the grid. Batteries are optional but help maximise self-consumption.

Q5. Will a 13.2kW system reduce my electricity bill to zero?

For many homes, yes. If your daily usage matches the output and you manage your consumption well, you could eliminate bills or even earn credits through feed-in tariffs.

Q6. Do I need three-phase power for a 13.2kW system?

While it’s not mandatory everywhere, three-phase power is often recommended or required for systems over 10kW to avoid export limits and improve performance.

Q7. How much roof space is needed for a 13.2kW solar system?

You’ll typically need 80 to 100 square metres of roof space, depending on the panel size and layout options available.

Q8. How long does installation take?

Most 13.2kW solar systems can be installed in 1–2 days, including setup, configuration, and grid connection.
