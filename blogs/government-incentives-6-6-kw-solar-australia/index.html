Federal Government Incentives

Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES)

The Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES) is one of the most significant government incentives for installing a 6.6kW solar system in Australia. This program offers Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs), which can be traded for cash. The number of STCs you can earn depends on your system size and location. A 6.6kW solar system can earn you up to $2,000 in rebates, significantly lowering the initial cost of installation.

Feed-in Tariffs (FiTs)

In addition to STCs, homeowners can also benefit from Feed-in Tariffs (FiTs). FiTs allow you to sell excess electricity generated by your solar system back to the grid. These payments, which vary by state, help reduce your overall electricity costs and provide additional income, making solar power even more cost-effective.
