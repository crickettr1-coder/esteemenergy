Rebates & Incentives for Solar Panel and Battery Systems

Australia offers several rebates and incentives that make investing in a solar panel and battery system more affordable:

  • Federal Battery Rebate (2025): $345 per kWh of usable battery capacity.

  • State-Specific Rebates:

    • WA: Interest-free loans to support battery installations

  • Virtual Power Plants (VPP): Earn extra income by selling excess energy stored in your battery back to the grid.

These incentives reduce the upfront cost of installing batteries for solar panels, making renewable energy more accessible to Australian households.
