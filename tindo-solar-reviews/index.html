Tindo Solar: Company Overview

Founded in 2011 and based in Adelaide, Tindo Solar has grown to become a leading name in Australian-made solar panels. They are the only company in Australia that manufactures solar panels locally, assembling high-quality products using global components. This unique position has allowed Tindo Solar to offer a combination of exceptional local craftsmanship and international technological expertise.

In addition to their innovative manufacturing processes, Tindo Solar has made a name for itself through a commitment to sustainability and local industry support. By choosing Tindo Solar, customers are not only ensuring high-performing solar systems for their homes, but also contributing to the growth of Australian manufacturing and renewable energy initiatives.

When reviewing how solar panels work, it’s clear that Tindo panels excel in both efficiency and durability. They offer various models, such as the Tindo Karra 72 Cell (400W), making them suitable for solar panels for home and commercial properties.

Tindo Karra 66 Cell (360W & 365W)

Durability and Warranty of Tindo Solar Panels

Tindo Solar panels are known for their long-lasting durability. Made with high-quality materials and designed for the Australian environment, these panels can withstand extreme conditions such as high heat, strong winds, and hail.

Tindo’s 25-year performance warranty ensures that the panels will maintain 80% of their original energy output even after 25 years. This long-term protection makes Tindo Solar an attractive option for homeowners looking for peace of mind and reliability.
