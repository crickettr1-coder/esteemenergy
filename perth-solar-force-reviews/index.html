Perth Solar Force is a renowned solar installation company based in Western Australia. What the Customers are Saying: A Review Summary

Perth Solar Force reviews often highlight positive customer experiences, especially regarding the quality of installation and the overall performance of the solar systems. Many customers report significant reductions in their energy costs after installation. Feedback generally focuses on the professionalism of the installation teams, the thoroughness of consultations, and the effectiveness of the solar panels.

However, there are some recurring mentions of delays in installation during peak seasons, as well as occasional challenges with post-installation support. Some customers have expressed that communication could be better, particularly when seeking assistance after the system has been installed.

Key Strengths Highlighted by Users

Based on a comprehensive review of Perth Solar Force reviews, here are some of the standout strengths that customers frequently highlight:

  • Quality of Installation: Many users praised the quality of the installation work, noting that Perth Solar Force’s team was efficient, professional, and careful in their approach to setting up the system.

  • Customer Service and Communication: While some users noted room for improvement in post-installation communication, others found the customer service team to be helpful, responsive, and informative throughout the initial consultation and installation phases.

  • System Performance: Perth Solar Force customers reported high satisfaction with the performance of their solar systems, with many saying that their energy costs were reduced significantly after installation. They found that systems like the 6-6 kW solar system offered impressive returns on investment.

  • Value for Money: Many users felt that Perth Solar Force provided great value for their investment, with competitive pricing for their high-quality systems. Comparing these costs with the 10 kW solar system from other providers shows a solid balance between price and quality.

Areas of Improvement (Based on Negative Feedback)

While the majority of Perth Solar Force reviews are positive, there are a few areas where customers have expressed concerns:

  • Post-installation Follow-up: Some customer feedback suggests that Perth Solar Force could improve in terms of follow-up communication once the system is installed. Several users mentioned that they had trouble reaching out for assistance after the initial installation was completed.

  • Communication During Peak Times: A few customers mentioned that the company’s communication could be better during busy periods, as delays in responses sometimes led to frustrations.

Addressing these concerns could improve their customer experience and solidify their standing as a top Perth solar installation company.
