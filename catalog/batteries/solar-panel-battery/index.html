            How do solar batteries function?

 

Solar batteries store excess electricity generated by solar panels for later use. Sunlight is converted into electricity by the panels, with surplus energy stored in the battery. During low solar generation, like at night or on cloudy days, the battery discharges stored energy to power your home, reducing grid reliance and providing backup during outages.

Why invest in a solar battery?

Solar batteries provide key benefits by storing energy for use at night or during outages, reducing grid reliance and lowering electricity costs. They can also help mitigate higher Time of Use (ToU) charges and offer backup power during blackouts. Additionally, participating in Virtual Power Plants can generate extra income by supporting the grid.
