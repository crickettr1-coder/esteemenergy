YOUR QUESTIONS ANSWERED

Frequently Asked Questions

Which solar system size is right for my home?

It depends on electricity use, roof space, shading, property type, phase supply and future energy plans. A property-specific assessment can help compare options.

What is the difference between 6.6kW and 10.5kW systems?

A 10.5kW system has more solar capacity and may suit a larger home or higher daytime energy use, subject to roof and electrical assessment.

Can I add a battery later?

A compatible battery may be added later, subject to the inverter, switchboard, wiring and the existing system design.

How much roof space is required?

The area depends on system size, panel model, roof layout, shading and access. The final requirement is confirmed during assessment.

Are larger systems suitable for every property?

No. Larger systems need suitable roof area, electrical infrastructure and consideration of local network requirements.

Does every battery provide blackout backup?

No. Backup requires compatible equipment, selected backup circuits and the correct installation configuration.

What affects solar installation cost?

Equipment selection, roof access, electrical work, property location, installation complexity and optional storage can all affect the final quote.

How long does installation take?

Timing varies with system size, roof condition, approvals, electrical work and property requirements.

Can the system be monitored?

Many compatible inverter and battery platforms provide monitoring, subject to the selected equipment.

Do I need a site assessment?

A site assessment is recommended to confirm suitability, system design, roof conditions, electrical requirements and installation planning.
