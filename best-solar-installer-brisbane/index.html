What to Look for in the Best Solar Installer Brisbane

Not all Brisbane solar companies are the same. Here’s what you should consider before choosing the best solar installer Brisbane:
  • Certified Solar Installers: Always select a CEC-accredited solar installer to ensure compliance with Australian industry standards.
  • Customer Reviews & Reputation: Look at Google ratings, customer feedback, and industry recognition to find a trustworthy installer.
  • Product Quality: Top installers provide high-efficiency solar panels from leading brands like Trina, Jinko, and Risen, along with premium inverters.
  • Warranty & After-Sales Support: Ensure your installer offers a 25-year solar panel warranty and 10+ years on workmanship.
  • Pricing & Financing Options: Compare quotes, check government rebates, and see if the company offers flexible payment plans.
  • Experience & Expertise: An installer with over 10 years of experience is more likely to provide a smooth and reliable solar installation.
